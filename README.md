# Alpha Rotation Lab

> Systematic ETF & Equity Rotation Strategies for A-Share and U.S. Markets

Alpha Rotation Lab is an open-source quantitative research organization focused on momentum rotation and adaptive asset allocation strategies.

We develop systematic, rule-based models across ETFs and equities, combining momentum ranking, trend persistence, and quantitative risk management.

# Strategy Philosophy

Momentum rotation is based on a persistent market phenomenon:

> Assets with strong relative strength tend to continue outperforming over intermediate time horizons.

Our models periodically rank assets by momentum and allocate capital toward the strongest performers, while dynamically adjusting exposure during weak or defensive market regimes.
