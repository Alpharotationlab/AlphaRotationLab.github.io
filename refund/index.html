Alpha Rotation Lab offers monthly subscriptions to digital research content. Because our service consists entirely of digital content that is made accessible immediately upon subscription confirmation, we have a limited refund policy as described below. 阿爾法輪動實驗室提供數字研究内容的月度訂閱服務。由於我們的服務完全由訂閱確認後立即可訪問的數字内容構成，我們的退款政策如下所述。

2. Monthly Subscription Refunds2. 月度訂閱退款

Refund requests may be considered within 7 days of the initial purchase date. If you are unsatisfied with the service for any reason within 7 days of your first subscription payment, please contact us and we will review your request. 退款申請可在首次購買日期後 7 天内提出。如果您在首次訂閱付款後 7 天内因任何原因對服務不滿意，請聯繫我們，我們將審核您的申請。

After the initial 7-day period, and due to the digital nature of the product: 在初始 7 天期限之後，由於產品的數字性質：

  • Refunds are generally not provided after content access has been granted, unless required by applicable consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction. 除非您所在司法管轄區的適用消費者保護法律另有要求，否則在授予内容訪問權限後通常不提供退款。

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation is processed through the Paddle billing portal or by contacting us directly. Upon cancellation: 您可隨時取消訂閱。取消通過 Paddle 帳單入口或直接聯繫我們處理。取消後：

  • Access to the private research portal will be removed at the end of the billing period. 計費週期結束時將撤銷對私有研究平台的訪問權限。
