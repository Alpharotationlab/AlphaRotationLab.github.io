Alpha Rotation Lab is a quantitative research collective formed by practitioners from U.S. and Chinese capital markets. We publish systematic, rules-based ETF model outputs — not investment advice, not personalised recommendations. 阿爾法輪動實驗室由中美資本市場資深從業者組成的量化研究團隊。我們發佈系統化、規則驅動的 ETF 模型輸出——不提供投資建議，不提供個性化推薦。

Research Philosophy研究理念

We believe robust investing
requires discipline over conviction. 我們相信穩健的投資
需要紀律勝過直覺

📐
Rules before prediction規則先於預測 We define rules in advance. We do not adjust models based on recent market events.我們事先定義規則，不根據近期市場事件調整模型。
🛡️
Risk control before return風險控制先於回報 Drawdown management and regime classification are core — not afterthoughts.回撤管理和市場狀態分類是核心，而非事後補充。
🔁
Consistency before optimisation一致性先於優化 The same process runs every week. No fitting to recent data, no changing the rules mid-cycle.每週相同的流程運行。不為近期數據過度優化，不在週期中改變規則。
⏳
Long-term discipline over short-term emotion長期紀律勝過短期情緒 We publish what the model says — not what the market is feeling this week.我們發佈模型的結果——而非本週市場的情緒感受。
Weekly Publishing Process每週發佈流程

The same five steps.
Every single week. 相同的五個步驟
每週雷打不動

9:00 AM ET
Every Tuesday每週二

Model outputs published to all subscribers simultaneously. No selective distribution. No early access. All subscribers receive identical content. 模型輸出同時發佈給所有訂閱者。無選擇性分發，無提前獲取，所有訂閱者收到相同内容。

1
Market Data Update市場數據更新
Latest price, volume and cross-asset data ingested for all ETFs in the universe.擷取宇宙中所有 ETF 的最新價格、成交量及跨資產數據。
2
Model Ranking Calculation模型排名計算
Multi-period momentum scores computed and ETFs ranked by relative strength across the full universe.計算多週期動量評分，依據全宇宙相對強弱對 ETF 進行排名。
3
Risk Regime Classification風險狀態分類
Market regime (Offensive / Neutral / Defensive) and risk score (0–100) determined from trend and volatility filters.根據趨勢與波動率過濾器確定市場狀態（進攻/中性/防守）及風險評分（0–100）。
4
Portfolio Rotation Output Generated輪動輸出結果生成
Final model output assembled: ETF rankings, regime signal, risk score, research commentary.最終模型輸出彙總：ETF 排名、市場狀態信號、風險評分、研究評論。
5
Uniform Publication to All Subscribers統一向全體訂閱者發佈
Research published to private Notion portal. All subscribers receive identical output simultaneously. Tuesday 9:00 AM ET.研究發佈至私有 Notion 平台。所有訂閱者同時收到相同輸出。週二美東 9:00。
